Help

    The Ratings site will create recommendations for you based on comparing your ratings of music, books and movies with other users' ratings. Ratings are cross-referenced across categories: if other users have book ratings similar to yours, this will affect your film and music recommendations and vice versa.

    Quickstart

    You need to register to be able to rate items and get recommendations.

    Search for the artist or band name you want to rate. If an album you want to rate is not listed, you can add it using the link on the bottom and then add a list of tracks with ratings. If it is listed, you can click on it and rate tracks / songs.

    There are no books or films in the database yet (21,000+ albums are already included). To rate books or films, you will first need to add them.

    On the bottom of search page, pick the type you want to add: artist / band, book author or director. You will see the add page with 15 rows of entry boxes where you can add 1-15 new authors at a time. After clicking "Add" button, you will see the list of links of authors you added. Clicking on a link will land you on a page where you can add books or movies. When you click on the add button you will again see 15 rows where you can enter names and ratings (0-100) of items.

    After adding a number of items and rating them, the site will calculate recommendations based on ratings by other users. This calculation is performed about once a day so you will need to check back on the next day to see updated recommendations. As more and more users add their ratings, the recommendations will become more accurate; to see them, click "My Recommendations" link on top of page.

    Misc. Notes

    If you add an item that's already present in the DB, it won't create a duplicate, as long as the name is exactly the same. When adding items, makes sure not to add an alternate spelling to the one that already there.

    Recommendation scores can be shown for all items, including the ones you rated. This will show how close the recommendation algorithm is to your own ratings.

    When calculating recommendations, the algorithm counts albums and films as being 10 times weightier than tracks and books are 4 times weightier than albums / films. If user A has the same rating for a movie as you do and user B has the same ratings for 10 songs as you, the algorithm will consider their ratings to be equally important for your recommendations.

    The search page currently searches for Artists / Bands, Authors and Directors. You can't search for film, book, album or track names. This will probably be added soon.

    You can add animation series or TV series as "films". More categories of items will be added at a later time: websites, manga, web and paper comics, audiobooks, etc.
